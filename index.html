Pia Pfeiffer

I’m a PhD student advised by Prof. Dr. Peter Filzmoser at the research unit Computational Statistics, Institute of Statistics & Mathematical Methods in Economics at TU Wien. My research interests include multivariate analysis and robust statistics.
